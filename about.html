About

I study neighborhood-scale urban air pollutant and greenhouse-gas emissions in the San Francisco Bay Area, Los Angeles, and Providence using dense sensor network observations (e.g., BEACO₂N), city activity data, and emissions inventories.

Current interests include: long-term sensor calibration, enhancement-ratio methods, urban CH₄ sources, and connecting diurnal/seasonal patterns to biogenic and anthropogenic fluxes.
