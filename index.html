Welcome

I study neighborhood-scale greenhouse gases and air pollutants in cities using dense stationary sensor networks, statistical modeling, and emissions inventories. My work develops calibration methods for long-term sensor deployments and uses enhancement ratios (ΔNOx/ΔCO₂, ΔCO/ΔCO₂, ΔCH₄/ΔCO₂) to infer sectoral emission factors across multiple U.S. cities.
