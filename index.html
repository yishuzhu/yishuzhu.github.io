About

I study neighborhood‑scale greenhouse gases and air pollutants in cities using dense stationary sensor networks, statistical modeling, and emissions inventories. My work develops calibration methods for long‑term sensor deployments and uses enhancement ratios (e.g., ΔNOx/ΔCO₂, ΔCO/ΔCO₂, ΔCH₄/ΔCO₂) to infer sectoral emission factors for transportation and residential energy use across multiple U.S. cities.

Research

Neighborhood‑scale urban emissions mapping — Deriving sectoral emission factors and city maps from BEACO₂N‑like networks; integrating advection–diffusion modeling and GIS.
